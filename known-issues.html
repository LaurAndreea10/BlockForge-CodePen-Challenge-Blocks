Known Issues & Compatibility

Transparent browser-specific limitations for BlockForge 5.5.

Screen recording

MediaRecorder and screen capture behavior differs by browser. Mobile Safari may not expose the same recording workflow as Chromium browsers.

Browser dependent
Voice commands and narration

Speech recognition is not available in every browser or language. Text narration can also use different system voices.

Progressive enhancement
Vibration

Haptic feedback is commonly available on Android browsers but may be unavailable on iOS.

Optional
PWA updates

An installed version may briefly show cached assets until the service worker activates. Closing and reopening the app applies the newest cache.

Expected PWA behavior
Large local backups

Very large galleries or replay histories are limited by the browser storage quota. Export backups regularly.

Storage dependent
